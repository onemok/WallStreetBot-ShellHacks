Simulation. Practice

Paper trading is simulated trading that allows an investor to practice buying and sell stocks without risking real money. Paper trading can be used to test a new strategy before you actually use it in your real account. It helps new traders navigate platforms and make trades, but since real money isn't involved, it doesn't fully represent real trading conditions.
